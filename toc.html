Annotated ECMAScript 5

The present document provides an annotated HTML view of the standard ECMA 262, edition 5, which is also known as ECMAScript Language Specification, 5th edition. The present document is not a normative version of the ECMAScript spec. Though it does include the full text of the spec, it is in fact a completely non-normative derivative work that is based on the spec—and is provided here strictly for the purpose of explaining the spec and assisting in its implementation.

No copyright is currently asserted on the annotations that are part of the present document. But the non-annotations part of the document is entirely verbatim text from the spec—used under the terms of that document’s copyright notice and license statement—so reuse of any non-annotation content from the present document must also adhere to the terms of that license.
